About

I am a research scientist at Google. I study machine learning for massive quantities of human activity data to provide actionable insights for decision making. Prior to Google, I was a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at Stanford University. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Sciences from the University of Wisconsin, Madison.
